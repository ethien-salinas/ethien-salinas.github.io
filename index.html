The evolution

My story in technology has been one of increasing scope: from enterprise engineering foundations to building fintech platforms from scratch, productizing financial operations, and leading digital banking systems in Europe.

Enterprise foundations

Built my early technical foundations in larger organizations, working with international teams, regulated systems and platform-level engineering.

Creze — digital lending platform

Moved from larger companies into startup execution, helping build the technology backbone of a working capital lender in Mexico.

Intelligential — fintech SaaS

Turned real lending operations into reusable financial infrastructure, building a SaaS platform used by financial institutions.

Íkualo — digital banking

Applied that platform and product experience to help build a financial service for migrants in Spain, operating in a regulated fintech environment.

How I lead

Technology must enable the business Platforms, architecture and delivery are only valuable if they improve how the business operates and grows.
Product and engineering evolve together In fintech, product decisions are deeply shaped by operational and technical reality. The best outcomes come when both evolve in sync.
Teams are part of the platform Systems scale better when the organization around them is clear, disciplined and able to grow with the company.
Reliability builds trust In financial systems, trust is earned through execution: consistency, controls, security and service quality.
